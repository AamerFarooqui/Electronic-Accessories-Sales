# 📊 Electronic Accessories Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Electronic Accessories Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across different electronic products, categories, and states. This interactive dashboard helps identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and key regional markets.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate **data visualization, business insights extraction, and dashboard development skills** using modern analytics tools.

---

## 🖼 Dashboard Preview

![Electronic Accessories Sales Dashboard](Dashboard%202.png)

---

## 🎯 Key Business Questions

This dashboard answers several important business questions:

* What is the **total revenue generated** from electronic accessories sales?
* Which **products generate the highest revenue**?
* Which **product categories contribute the most to revenue**?
* Which **states have the highest sales performance**?
* What is the **trend of revenue across quarters and days**?
* How much revenue is **lost due to order cancellations**?

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

The dashboard highlights the following KPIs:

* 💰 **Total Revenue:** ₹1.25 Billion
* 🛒 **Total Sales:** ₹1.77 Billion
* 📊 **Average Order Value:** ₹112.85K
* ❌ **Lost Revenue due to Cancellation:** ₹525.42 Million

These metrics provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Revenue by Product

Displays revenue generated by each product.

Key insights:

* **MacBook Air** and **OnePlus 9** are the top revenue-generating products.
* **Realme Cable** contributes the least revenue.

---

### 2️⃣ Revenue by Category

Shows how different product categories contribute to total revenue.

Insights:

* **Laptop category generates the highest revenue**
* Followed by **Mobile and Headphones**

---

### 3️⃣ Revenue by State

Visualizes sales performance across different states.

Top-performing states:

* Maharashtra
* Gujarat
* Rajasthan

These regions represent the strongest markets for electronic accessories.

---

### 4️⃣ Revenue Trend by Day & Quarter

A time-series visualization showing revenue fluctuations across quarters.

Insights:

* Revenue trends show **periodic fluctuations across quarters**
* Certain quarters show **strong spikes in sales performance**

---

## 🧠 Business Insights

* **Laptop and Mobile categories dominate overall revenue**
* **MacBook Air is the best performing product**
* **Maharashtra and Gujarat are major revenue-driving markets**
* A significant **₹525M revenue loss occurs due to order cancellations**, indicating an opportunity to optimize order fulfillment.
